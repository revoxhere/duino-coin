Let's go!

Let's get started!

Firstly, You need to download latest release for your OS.
Click on that big button below that will take you to the releases page.
Scroll down a bit and you should see download links.
If you're using Windows - download Windows version. If Linux (or OSX) - Linux version.
Simple as that.

Latest release

After you've downloaded latest release, unzip it for example to your Desktop.

Next step

Register on the Duino-Coin network

Open found inside the release you just unzipped.
You're quite likely new here so click the button.
Create a cool username and a strong password.
Then register your account.

After successful registration you can login.
Enter your account credentials, click and...

Next step

Using the Wallet

You should now see your Wallet window.
From here you can watch your balance grow, monitor DUCO price, your funds to another cryptocurrency, tweak some , and to someone.

Next step

Mine using PC

Let's start making some money.
Launch from your Duino-Coin directory.
Miner will show you everything you need to know and guide you through basic configuration.
After you'll set it up, it'll remember the settings and will automatically mine every time you open it again.
You can open your Wallet to check how much you're getting rewarded for accepted shares.

Next step

Mine using Raspberry Pi

To mine with this small computer you'll need to download latest release for Linux.
Unpack it to a safe place.

Open Terminal and navigate to your Duino-Coin directory using cd command.
After successfully navigating to it, launch PC Miner using python3 PC_Miner.py.

The rest is the same as you would use PC Miner on typical computer.
Miner will show you everything you need to know and guide you through basic configuration.
After you'll set it up, it'll remember the settings and will automatically mine every time you open it again.
You can open your Wallet to check how much you're getting rewarded for accepted shares.

Next step

Mine using ESP8266/32

Using ESP boards you can mine without need of a computer.
Open folder in which you should find file.
Open it using Arduino IDE.
Near the top you should see WiFi and Duino-Coin credentials fields.

Fill them according to your WiFi network and Duino-Coin account.
Upload the code to your board just like you've uploaded your LED Blink codes in the past.

After successfuly uploading the code, you can open in Arduino IDE to check if everything is working.

When ESP finds a share, It'll blink its built-in LED two times.
You can open your to check how much you're getting rewarded for accepted shares.
If your balance is rising, GG! Your ESP is now making money! ðŸŽ‰

Next step

Mine using Android smartphone

Mining using Smartphone is the hardest to set-up but after following these steps you should be able to achieve it.

Firstly, download an app called PyDroid3 from Google Play which we'll use to run Python.
Then you need to download Minimal-PC_Miner and open it with PyDroid.

You'll need to replace username and password lines near the top of the file with your credentials.

After changing them, click the yellow arrow on bottom of the screen.
If you've entered everything correctly your smartphone will now be mining Duino-Coins! ðŸŽ‰

Next step

Mine using your Internet Browser

You can now mine Duino-Coin with any modern web browser (mine on smartphones or other internet enabled devices without need of installing DUCO software).

To do it, visit Duino-Coin Webminer page and enter your Duino-Coin credentials.
Click the "Start" button and wait for a few seconds to let the JS communicate with the proxy server.
If you've entered everything correctly your browser will now be mining Duino-Coins! ðŸŽ‰
